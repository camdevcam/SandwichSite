About the Owner: Wilber Porkins. Weird name, serious sandwich maker.

Mr. Wilber Porkins, owner of Stuff’N’Face Sandwich Shops grew up with a dream of making people happy by offering them good food even on short notice. Actualizing his dream with the opening of the Stuff’N’Face franchise, Mr. Porkins revels as he hears positive feedback from his many happy customers. Wilber Porkins’s drive to commitment is what brought this shop into a reality and draws hundreds of people each and every day.
